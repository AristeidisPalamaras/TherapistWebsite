Curriculum Vitae

I am a psychologist, a person-centred counsellor, and a trauma therapist.

Having both a bachelor’s and a master's degree in maritime studies, I worked for several years in a shipping company.

In 2009 however, my personal quests led me down a new path. Thus, I obtained a bachelor’s degree in Psychology (BSc in Psychology, Open University) and a master's degree in Person-Centred Counselling (MSc in Person-Centred Counselling, University of Strathclyde).

I have also been trained in the trauma therapy methods of EMDR from the Trauma Therapy Institute of Greece and IFS (Pre-Level One Training) by Dr. Alexia Rothman and Trauma2therapy

I conduct individual sessions and facilitate personal development groups, as well as groups combining creative writing with the therapeutic process.

I have also written three book of fiction, which have been published by Kedros Publications.
