Kostas Mavrakakis

My name is Kostas Mavrakakis and I am a psychologist, person-centred counsellor, and trauma therapist trained in EMDR and IFS (Pre-Level One).

My office is located in the center of Athens (near the Acropolis metro station), and I offer individual sessions both in person and online.

You may be considering starting sessions due to a specific difficulty or challenge you are facing, or due to a general feeling that things in your life aren't quite how you would like them to be.

In any case, my role is to support and assist you in exploring your thoughts and emotions in order to build a more fulfilling life.

I offer a ten-minute phone call where I can answer your questions, as well as a first session free of charge for you to see whether my approach suits you - and for me to see if I can help you.
